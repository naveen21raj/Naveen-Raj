Women's Dresses on Amazon

That feeling you get when you slip on the perfect dress? Amazon Fashion lives for it. That’s why we stock our Dress Shop with runway-worthy trends and test-of-time classics to make it easy to fall in love with your best dress for any occasion.

Our Dress Shop boasts a wide selection of silhouettes from maxis to minis, mild-mannered shifts and sweet sundresses to body con stunners and fit-and-flare cocktail frocks. On weekends, let your hair down in T-shirt dresses in soft jersey knit or dreamy boho-inspired dresses in worldly patterns. When Monday rolls around, put your best foot forward in a timeless wrap dress or a polished sheath that goes effortlessly from desk to dinner. Friday night options are as varied as your plans from romantic lace to dazzling sequins.

Going to the chapel? Say “yes” to wear-again bridesmaids’ dresses, mother-of-the-bride offerings, gorgeous wedding gowns, and wedding-guest dresses that look as good at the ceremony as they do at the after party. With brands like BCBGeneration, Adrianna Papel, Taylor, Ellen Tracy, Vince Camuto, Maggy London, and more, you’re sure to find a dress well suited to your next celebration. Amazon even has exclusive brands you can’t get anywhere else like Ella Moon and Lark & Ro.

With dresses in an array of colors, lengths, and fabrics in misses, plus, and petite sizes, Amazon has the pick to make you look feel a million bucks for far less. And we offer free shipping and returns on hundreds of dresses, so you can try on multiple styles and sizes at home and easily send back the ones that don’t speak to you. Just look for items sold and shipped by Amazon.com.

Whether you’re looking to brunch in style or glam it up at an upcoming gala, Amazon delivers the perfect dress—and that perfect-dress feeling—right to your door.
